Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description

If you're looking for a super easy and healthy way to start your day, look no further than this fruity sustenance.

Please note you will need a blender with the power to crush ice

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1 Pint Strawberries
  • 3 cups yogurt (unflavored or vanilla)
  • 3 cups ice
  • 3 cups milk of your choice (dairy, oat, soy, etc)
  • 1 scoop protein powder of your choice

Steps

  1. Cut bananas into quarters and add to blender
  2. Remove tops from strawberries and discard; add berries to blender
  3. Add ice, protein powder, yogurt and milk to blender
  4. run blender on high pulse in 10 second intervals until smoothie reaches desired consistency. Note: Due to the differences in consistencies of ingredients it may be necessary to stop and stir the smoothie for optimal blending
