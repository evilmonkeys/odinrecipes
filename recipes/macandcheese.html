Kraft Maccaroni and Cheese

Description

The ubiquitous blue box of maccaroni and cheese was a staple for many North American kids growing up. Regardless of the brand, the tall, rectangular box with that unmistakable rattle told you what was for lunch.

Sometimes the simplicity of the childhood meals are all we need to feel nourished and sustained

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Maccaroni and Cheese (brand of your choice)
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 lump of butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan bring 6 cups water to a boil.
  2. Once water is at a rolling boil, add pasta from the box of maccaroni and cheese, ensuring you keep the pouch of cheese seperate
  3. Boil pasta 8-10 minutes, or until al dente
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Return pasta to saucepan
  6. Combine milk, cheese powder and butter with the pasta in pan
  7. Mix well
