Grilled Cheese

Description

Sometimes, you're hungry as all hell, but you don't have the gumption nor the time to get gourmet. The options for a grilled cheese are nearly limitless, but we're going to start about as basic as you can get so there's nowhere to go but up

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of plain white bread
  • 1-2 slices of plain yellow american cheese
  • Butter or margarine

Instructions

  1. Preheat medium skillet over medium-low heat
  2. Butter both sides of each slice of bread
  3. Once skillet is hot, place one slice of bread on the skillet
  4. Place cheese on that slice of bread, followed by other buttered slice of bread
  5. Cook over low-medium heat until bread is golden brown
  6. Flip sandwich with a spatula
  7. When other side is brown and cheese is melted, remove sandwich from stove and place on a plate
  8. Eat the hell outta that sumbitch
